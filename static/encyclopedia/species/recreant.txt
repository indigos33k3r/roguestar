Throughout the galaxy, wherever long-extinct spacefaring civilizations have waged war, obsolete battle robots known as recreants remain behind.
They can be seen patrolling scorched battlefields with little sense of purpose, and continue to be constructed by autonomous, self-repairing underground factories.
On some worlds Recreants have overcome their innate and often designed-in limitations to establish something resembling healthy civilization.
Most continue to mindlessly carry out the final instructions of their extinct masters.
