Throughout the galaxy, wherever long-extinct spacefaring civilizations have waged war, obsolete battle robots known as recreants remain behind.
To this day self-sustaining underground factories continue to manufacture them in untold quantities.  They can be seen patrolling scorched battlefields with little sense of purpose.
On some worlds recreants have overcome their innate limitations to establish something resembling sustainable civilization.
Most continue to mindlessly carry out the final instructions of their extinct masters.
